Region traffic dispersion measurement results.

Overview

This is a measurement of the traffic dispersion of a region with a rate limiter.

Description of the data used for the measurement

Three regions were used for the measurement. The characteristics of each region are as follows

region quota duration
us-west1 100 times 60 sec.
us-west2 100 times 60 sec.
us-west3 50 times 60 sec.
  • quota: Requests per duration(s) limit.
  • duration: Request limit duration(seconds).

To verify that traffic is properly distributed among the three regions, traffic is sent with the following contents.

  • In all, the traffic is sent 1,875 times.
  • Traffic is generated approximately 10.4 times per second.

Measurement results

Below is a graph of the measurement results, where the Y-axis is the number of traffic performed and the X-axis is the time the traffic was requested.

The graph shows that traffic is appropriately distributed by region.
Since us-west1 and us-west2 have the same quota and duration, we can see that the traffic is approximated.
The us-west3 has half the quota compared to the other two regions, so the traffic is similarly halved.
